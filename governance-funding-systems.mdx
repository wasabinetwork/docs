## Core Offerings

### Governance & Operations

**When governance becomes real, not aspirational.**

I help ecosystems move from informal coordination to accountable, resilient governance — without over-engineering the system or breaking community trust.

#### What This Includes

- Governance system design and restructuring
- Council, committee, and working group design
- Multisig setup, signer coordination, and operational processes
- Governance audits and failure-mode analysis
- Delegate programs and voter coordination
- Proposal workflows and decision-making processes
- Translating governance frameworks into daily operations
- Governance retrospectives and process improvements

#### Ideal For

- DAOs scaling treasury size or responsibility
- Protocols formalizing governance beyond early-stage coordination
- Ecosystems experiencing governance friction or fatigue
- Public goods organizations requiring legitimacy and trust
- Teams preparing for councils, delegates, or multisig operations

<Tip>
  Good governance is not just about voting. It is about designing clear roles, trusted processes, accountable execution, and healthy escalation paths before the system is under pressure.
</Tip>

### Grants Programs & Funding Operations

**From funding intent to fair, high-signal allocation.**

I design and operate grants programs that are credible, transparent, and scalable — minimizing noise while maximizing signal and impact.

#### What This Includes

- Grants round design
- Program scope, incentives, and eligibility criteria
- Council and reviewer coordination
- Reviewer calibration and quality assurance
- Application review pipelines
- Conflict-of-interest detection and mitigation
- Round operations using tools like Charmverse and similar platforms
- Matching funds allocation frameworks
- Post-round reporting, learnings, and retrospectives

#### Ideal For

- Protocols launching or refining grants programs
- Ecosystems running recurring funding rounds
- Funds allocating capital to OSS, ReFi, or IRL impact
- Communities that need transparent review and allocation processes
- Programs trying to reduce noise, gaming, or low-quality applications

<Check>
  A strong grants program does more than distribute money. It creates legitimacy, reveals ecosystem priorities, and helps communities learn how to fund what matters.
</Check>

## How Engagements Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Context Review">
    We clarify your ecosystem, governance model, funding structure, current challenges, stakeholders, and desired outcomes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Diagnosis">
    I identify system gaps, governance debt, misaligned incentives, operational risks, and areas where coordination may break down.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design">
    I help design the governance process, grants workflow, funding framework, or advisory path needed for your situation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Implementation">
    Depending on the engagement, I support execution through operations, documentation, reviewer coordination, council support, or process rollout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Learning">
    We close the loop with retrospectives, reporting, process improvements, and clear next steps for long-term resilience.
  </Step>
</Steps>
