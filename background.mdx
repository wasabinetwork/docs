> Founder, Ecosystem Operator & Public Goods Builder

I’m a founder and ecosystem operator focused on building real-world solutions for systemic funding challenges.

I currently serve as **Secretary of [Kokonut Network](https://kokonut.network)**, a blockchain-enabled ecosystem powered by **trees and people**, designed to fund, govern, and scale regenerative projects in underserved communities.

Alongside Kokonut, I’ve been deeply involved in the **Web3 public goods and governance space since 2021**, contributing to founder circles, working groups, grants programs, and ecosystem funding frameworks — including the [**EBF Framework**](https://ebfcommons.org/), **Gitcoin Community Council**, and the **RWA x ReFi Report**.

<Info>
  My work sits at the intersection of governance, public goods funding, regenerative finance, and real-world impact.
</Info>

---

## Mission & Perspective

I’m a **Public Goods maximalist and regeneration practitioner** at heart.

My work is driven by a clear problem:

> Millions of impactful, real-world initiatives remain unfunded because they do not meet the risk profiles or structural requirements of legacy institutions.

[Kokonut Network](https://kokonut.network) exists as a direct response to this gap.

For me, this work is not about building another startup. It is about **challenging the status quo**, designing alternative funding primitives, and proving that community-aligned systems can sustainably support real-world impact at scale.

Each entity within the Kokonut ecosystem is meant to act as a signal: that better coordination, governance, and capital allocation models are possible.

<Check>
  The goal is simple: help communities fund what matters, govern responsibly, and scale impact without losing trust.
</Check>
