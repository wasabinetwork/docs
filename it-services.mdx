### Custom Corporate Emails

A professional email makes your business look more trustworthy.

I help set up custom email addresses using your domain, such as `hello@yourdomain.com`, `support@yourdomain.com`, or `name@yourdomain.com`.

**Corporate Email Services Include**

- Custom domain email setup
- Email inbox configuration
- DNS records for email
- SPF, DKIM, and DMARC basics
- Email aliases and forwarding
- Team inbox structure
- Migration from personal email accounts
- Basic deliverability review

<Warning>
  Using personal email addresses for business can reduce trust and create operational confusion as your team grows.
</Warning>

<Check>
  **Best Fit**

  This is ideal for businesses, founders, teams, and organizations that want to present themselves professionally and centralize communication within a single domain.
</Check>
